KANANG-ALALAY SYSTEM
## Complete User + Staff + Admin Interaction Guide (Public to Internal Flow) ---
# SYSTEM OVERVIEW
The **Kanang-Alalay System** has **3 main user levels**:
1. **Public Users (Visitors / Donors / Organizations)** 2. **Staff (Nurse / Caregiver)**
3. **Admin (Management System)**
The system starts from a **public homepage** and transitions into **secure internal dashboards**. ---
# **PUBLIC WEBSITE (HOME PAGE)** ## When Opened:
* Displays:
* Facility background (image/video) * Welcome message
* Mission/Vision of Kanang-Alalay
---
## Main Sections:
### Home
* Overview of facility
* About services (elderly care, outreach, etc.)
---
### Booking
For scheduling:
* Outreach programs * Facility visits
* Medical missions
---
### Donation
For:
* Food donations
